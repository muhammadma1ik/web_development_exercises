Spaghetti Carbonara

This classic Italian dish is rich, creamy, and full of flavor. Perfect for a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • 50g parmesan cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

Method

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs in a bowl, then mix in the cheeses and pepper.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and mix quickly with the pancetta and egg mixture off the heat.
  5. Serve immediately with extra cheese and pepper on top.
