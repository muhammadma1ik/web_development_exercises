Characters

Walter White: A brilliant chemistry teacher turned methamphetamine manufacturer whose descent into the criminal underworld defines the series.

Jesse Pinkman: Walter’s former student and reluctant partner who struggles with his conscience amid the chaos of the drug trade.

Skyler White: Walter’s wife, who faces her own challenges as she grapples with the moral implications of her husband’s secret life.

Hank Schrader: A determined DEA agent whose pursuit of the elusive drug kingpin adds intense pressure and danger to the storyline.

Saul Goodman: A charismatic lawyer who provides both comic relief and critical assistance to Walter and Jesse in their dealings with the criminal world.

The layered personalities and evolving dynamics between these characters contribute significantly to the series’ depth and tension.
